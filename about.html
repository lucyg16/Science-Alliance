About Us

Interested in signing up? Click here for more details.

Recognized by the both the Prudential Spirit of the Community Award and the President of the United States, Science Alliance is an after-school enrichment program, serving over 200 students from 22 elementary schools in the San Ramon Valley. In the current technological era, it has become increasingly important that we build a strong science foundation in the youth in order to shape the thinkers, creators, and innovators of tomorrow.

Founded with a mission to introduce elementary school students to investigative science and methodical inquiry, Science Alliance pairs motivated elementary school students with high school mentors (from Monte Vista, San Ramon Valley, Dougherty Valley, & California High Schools), who teach the scientific process while guiding the students through original science fair projects.

"I've never thought of myself as a science person, but I LOVED working with Carrie! Even though Science Alliance is over, we still get to talk a lot. No matter what, I definitely want to be a Science Alliance mentor someday."

-Alexandra (5th grade Science Alliance Buddy)

Both the kids and mentors commit to this yearlong program that helps prepare the kids for middle and high school lab science and gives the mentors the opportunity to act as positive role models for younger students.

"I have seen many science fair programs for kids, but none with so many facilitation elements embedded in the mentoring process to help kids not only learn science, but also have a great time. All this culminating in a huge science fair is just fantastic! I would definitely like to see this model replicated throughout the state of California"

-Emmanuel Akanyrige Diablo Valley College Professor Science Alliance Parent 2007

Science Alliance holds meetings once a month for the mentors and buddies to meet and discuss and work on their experiments. Each get-together has a fun theme, whether itâ€™s the December Holiday Cookie Exchange or the March Ice Cream Social. The big finale is the April Science Extravaganza which includes the actual science fair, exciting activity stations, special presentations , and the highly anticipated awards ceremony (with past prizes including Six Flags tickets, movie theater passes, and Cold Stone gift certificates).

We encourage all 5th graders, whether they are required to do a science fair project by their teacher or not, to participate. Science Alliance is also a great opportunity for high schoolers who are passionate about science and love kids to do a great community service project. Overall, Science Alliance is a super fun experience that is rewarding to everyone involved.
