FAQ

How do I sign my child up for Science Alliance?

Make sure to attend one of the four mandatory parent meetings held in September. At the parent informational meetings, the officers and teachers will provide you with more information about the club and how to sign up for the program.

How do I register for a Parent Meeting?

No registrations for the meetings are required, just go to any you choose. Each of the meetings is held in the Dougherty Valley High School Commons.

How does registration work (after the Parent Meetings)?

A link to register will be posted on the homepage of the designated high school. Registration will open at a certain time and date in late September or early October (announced at parent meetings) and is done by a first come, first serve basis.

Does my child need to be assigned a science fair project to participate?

No, any 5th grade SRVUSD student who wants to participate in the program can, and no preference will be given to students that are assigned projects by their school.

Does Science Alliance cost money?

There is no charge for Science Alliance, but there is a voluntary donation of $45 after the parent meeting for the cost of the food and materials (T-shirt, binder, printing, custodians, Science Fair costs) provided in the program. All teachers and students involved in the program are volunteers.

Does Science Alliance accept special education students?

Science Alliance accepts all SRVUSD 5th graders, including special education students.
